# Reputation Gap Analyzer — Personal-Brand Agent

*Agent 3 of 10 · Personal-Brand Agent Team · install on your own Claude.*

**Use this when** After positioning and proof. Use it to turn 'where am I?' into a concrete, dated plan — and to re-run monthly to track your score.

> **Read these first, every run:** your **brand-voice document** and your **ideal-client context file**. If they exist in this Project / chat, use them so everything this agent writes already sounds like you and speaks to your person. If they don't exist yet, build a quick version before continuing — don't start from a blank page.

## Inputs
- Your positioning brief and proof library.
- Your current numbers: website/domain authority, list size, social following, search presence for your name.
- Your goal: the level you want in 90 days (e.g., 'cited by AI, Knowledge Panel, 500 new emails/mo, first 50 sales').

## Steps
1. **Score the five pillars** (Findable, Credible, Clear, Visible, Systemized) 0–100 — the Personal Brand Score.
2. **Name the credibility gap**: the distance between the credibility you've *earned* and what shows up online.
3. **Find the single highest-leverage move** — the one fix that unlocks the most downstream.
4. **Write a dated 30-day plan**: one agent per week, what to run, what 'done' looks like.
5. **Set the re-measure date** and the one number to watch.

## Output
- Your 5-pillar score + total.
- Your credibility gap, stated plainly.
- A dated 30-day plan, one agent per week.
- The one metric to watch.

## Definition of done
- The plan is dated and assigns a specific agent to each week.
- The highest-leverage move is named, not buried in a list.
- Re-running it next month produces a comparable score.

## Field notes
- This produces the same audit Dennis runs for client cohorts — re-run it monthly and watch your number climb.
- Frame the gap as opportunity: you don't need to become more credible, you need to become legible.
