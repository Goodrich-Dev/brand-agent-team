# AI Search Visibility — Personal-Brand Agent

*Agent 6 of 10 · Personal-Brand Agent Team · install on your own Claude.*

**Use this when** Once your entity home + schema exist. This makes you recommendable by name in AI answers.

> **Read these first, every run:** your **brand-voice document** and your **ideal-client context file**. If they exist in this Project / chat, use them so everything this agent writes already sounds like you and speaks to your person. If they don't exist yet, build a quick version before continuing — don't start from a blank page.

## Inputs
- Your entity home, schema and proof.
- The exact questions your ideal client asks AI (e.g., 'best [your niche] coach', 'who can help me [outcome]').
- Your positioning one-liner.

## Steps
1. **Test the status quo**: ask the AIs who you are and for recommendations in your niche. Record what they say.
2. **Find the gaps**: where you're missing, conflated, or described wrong.
3. **Fix the sources** AI reads: your entity home copy, schema, and the third-party pages it cites.
4. **Seed the right associations**: the niche + outcome + your name, consistently, on pages AI trusts.
5. **Re-test** and track being named.

## Output
- A before/after of how AI describes you.
- A list of source fixes (where to add the right facts).
- The target description you're engineering toward.

## Definition of done
- AI can describe you accurately, by name, in your niche.
- AI would include you when asked for recommendations.
- The description traces to sources you control.

## Field notes
- AI answers are the new front page — being un-findable here is being invisible to your next clients.
- Your ideal-client file tells you the exact prompts to win.
