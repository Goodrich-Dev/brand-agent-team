# Knowledge Panel Entity SEO — Personal-Brand Agent

*Agent 5 of 10 · Personal-Brand Agent Team · install on your own Claude.*

**Use this when** After your entity home exists. This is how you become an entity Google and AI can cite by name.

> **Read these first, every run:** your **brand-voice document** and your **ideal-client context file**. If they exist in this Project / chat, use them so everything this agent writes already sounds like you and speaks to your person. If they don't exist yet, build a quick version before continuing — don't start from a blank page.

## Inputs
- Your entity home URL and proof library.
- Your sameAs list: every profile, podcast, press, author page.
- Your credentials and any Wikipedia/Wikidata-eligible facts.

## Steps
1. **Audit your entity**: does Google already have an object for your name? Is it the right one? Any namesake confusion?
2. **Add Person schema** to your entity home (name, job title, credentials, sameAs, image).
3. **Strengthen third-party signals**: consistent name+role everywhere, author bylines, podcast/press links pointing home.
4. **Map the path to Wikidata/Knowledge Panel** eligibility and what proof is still needed.
5. **Verify** in Google Search Console + Knowledge Graph.

## Output
- Person schema for your entity home.
- An entity audit (what Google knows, namesake risks).
- A dated path-to-Panel checklist.

## Definition of done
- Person schema validates.
- Your name resolves to YOU, not a namesake.
- Every signal points to one entity home.

## Field notes
- Knowledge Panels follow entity clarity, not vanity — one consistent name+role+home beats scattered profiles.
- A panel is within reach for anyone with the same foundation: a clean entity with real third-party signals.
