# Content Factory — Personal-Brand Agent

*Agent 8 of 10 · Personal-Brand Agent Team · install on your own Claude.*

**Use this when** Weekly, once positioning is set. The engine that keeps your hub fed without daily effort.

> **Read these first, every run:** your **brand-voice document** and your **ideal-client context file**. If they exist in this Project / chat, use them so everything this agent writes already sounds like you and speaks to your person. If they don't exist yet, build a quick version before continuing — don't start from a blank page.

## Inputs
- One source asset: a video, a livestream, a talk, or a long voice note (use Wispr Flow to just talk).
- Your brand-voice document and ideal-client file.
- Your content pillars (from positioning).

## Steps
1. **Transcribe and mine** the source for the 3–5 best ideas.
2. **Spin formats**: a blog post (definitive where it fits), a podcast description, 3–5 short-form scripts, social captions — all in your voice.
3. **Hook for your ideal client**, not for everyone.
4. **Point each piece home** — link to the entity home / freebie.
5. **Queue and repurpose**: one input → a week of output; save the best to feed Dollar a Day.

## Output
- A week of content from one input: blog, podcast notes, 3–5 shorts, captions.
- Each piece on-voice and pointing to your lead magnet/home.
- Your best piece flagged for $1/day.

## Definition of done
- Everything reads like you (brand-voice file used).
- One input produced a full week.
- Every piece links back to the hub.

## Field notes
- This is your Content Agent — your first AI employee. Wispr Flow means you can talk, not type, your source.
- Repurpose, don't reinvent: one video is a week. That's how visibility runs without daily effort.
