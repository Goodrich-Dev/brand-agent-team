# Personal Brand Website Agent — Personal-Brand Agent

*Agent 4 of 10 · Personal-Brand Agent Team · install on your own Claude.*

**Use this when** Once you have positioning + proof. Your entity home is the hub every other spoke points back to.

> **Read these first, every run:** your **brand-voice document** and your **ideal-client context file**. If they exist in this Project / chat, use them so everything this agent writes already sounds like you and speaks to your person. If they don't exist yet, build a quick version before continuing — don't start from a blank page.

## Inputs
- Your positioning brief, proof library, and hero proofs.
- Your name domain (buy yourname.com if you don't own it).
- Your freebie/lead magnet and your entry-offer link.

## Steps
1. **Structure the home around your name** as the entity: who you help, your method, your proof, your offers.
2. **Put your lead-magnet opt-in above the fold** — the email capture is the first conversion.
3. **Make the entry offer the obvious next click** after the lead magnet — no call required.
4. **Add the facts machines need**: a clear bio, credentials, links to every profile and proof (sameAs).
5. **Link the spokes home**: every social profile, podcast and guest post points back here.

## Output
- A page plan + drafted copy for your entity home.
- Above-the-fold lead-magnet opt-in + clear path to the course.
- A sameAs link list (every profile/proof) for schema.

## Definition of done
- A stranger knows in 5 seconds who you help and what to buy.
- Your name — not just your brand — is the site's subject.
- The lead-magnet→email→offer path is unmistakable.

## Field notes
- If you have a brand name (not your name) like a business, still build the entity home at YOUR name and link the two — the person is the asset that compounds.
- Keep it facts-first; this is the page the Knowledge Panel agent will point Google to.
