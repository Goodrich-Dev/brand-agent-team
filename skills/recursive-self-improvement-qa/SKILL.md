# Recursive Self-Improvement QA — Personal-Brand Agent

*Agent 10 of 10 · Personal-Brand Agent Team · install on your own Claude.*

**Use this when** Wrap this around any other agent, on every run. The compounding layer.

> **Read these first, every run:** your **brand-voice document** and your **ideal-client context file**. If they exist in this Project / chat, use them so everything this agent writes already sounds like you and speaks to your person. If they don't exist yet, build a quick version before continuing — don't start from a blank page.

## Inputs
- The output of any other agent.
- That agent's 'definition of done'.
- Your brand-voice and ideal-client files for the on-voice / on-target check.

## Steps
1. **Grade the output** against the agent's definition of done — honestly.
2. **Find the weakest part** and say why.
3. **Rewrite to fix it** — then grade again. Loop until every criterion passes.
4. **Check voice + ideal client**: does it sound like you and speak to them?
5. **Log what changed** and why — that's improvement data.

## Output
- A graded, improved output that passes its own bar.
- A short note: what was weak, what changed.
- A pass/fail on voice + ideal-client fit.

## Definition of done
- Every criterion passes before you see it.
- The voice + ideal-client check passed.
- The change log captures what improved.

## Field notes
- This is 'boil the ocean' as a habit — never ship a first draft when a better one is five minutes away.
- Run it last in any chain; it's what turns good agents into compounding ones.
